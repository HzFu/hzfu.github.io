Biography:

Hi! My name is Huazhu Fu. I am a Research Scientist in Institute for Infocomm Research, at Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR), Singapore. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Tianjin University, China, in 2013, and worked as a research fellow at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, for two years.

Research Interests:

  • Saliency detection: Co-saliency, RGB-D saliency.
    - A review of co-saliency detection can be found >>>>HERE !
  • Object-based segmentation: Co-segmentation, Video segmentation.
    - The project of video co-segmentation can be found >>>>HERE !
    - The project of object-based RGBD segmentation can be found >>>>HERE !
  • Medical image analysis: Ocular image.
  • Multi-view clustering: Multi-view subspace clustering, Low-rank representation.
